# Steps to Configure BitBucket insights

Before you begin, ensure the BitBucket Integration feature is enabled for your project. Go to “Settings” in the left sidebar and under **“Features”**, enable the **“Support for BitBucket and GitLab”** feature.

<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-integration.png"
  alt="bitbucket-integration"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>

#### 1. Select the provider your app is configured with.

- Navigate to the Insights tab in the left sidebar of the SkyU Console.
- Click **BitBucket** sidebar item.
- If your BitBucket app is not configured, you will see the below box to configure insights.
- Click the **+ Configurations** button.

<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-configurations.png"
  alt="bitbucket-configurations"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>

- Now you will be directed to Insights’ Settings page. If you have your app added already, you will see it listed under BitBucket tab.

<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-tab.png"
  alt="bitbucket-tab"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>

- You will see your configured organization / workspace name, added apps and added custom apps for the insights configurations. ( Added apps through creating apps within the console and Custom apps through the “Add Custom Apps” button in Settings pages )
- Click **“Configure Bitbucket"** button to start the insights configurations.

#### 2. Configure BitBucket data collection ( For BitBucket connected apps )

- **Create your BitBucket App Password** [How to get your App Password](https://support.atlassian.com/bitbucket-cloud/docs/create-an-app-password/) with the given permissions which are under “Included Permissions” and enter that App Password value in the “Password Verification” tab’s Password input.

<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-pat.png"
  alt="bitbucket-pat"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>

- After the validation is successful, You will be taken to the “Issues” tab and scroll down to click the “Next” button.
- Click the “Next” button in the “Tags” tab.
- Click the “Create” button in the “Cross-Domain” tab.
- Once it's configured successfully, you will get the success message and “Done” button to complete the configurations.

<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-final-configurations.png"
  alt="bitbucket-final-configurations"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>

- You will be redirected to the Settings page where you can see that the app is configured to use its insights.

<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-configured.png"
  alt="bitbucket-configured"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>

- Click the **“Sync With Devlake”** button to sync with the latest data in your configured repository.
- Now you can check the Gitbub sidebar item.

<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-insights.png"
  alt="bitbucket-insights"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>

#### 3. Custom BitBucket app configuration

- Click **“+ Add Custom Apps”** button to create your custom app insights configuration in the “Insights Settings” page.

<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-custom.png"
  alt="bitbucket-custom"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>

- Click "Auth with BitBucket" and after the popup, scroll down where you can select the BitBucket workspaces, repositories and branches.

<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-custom-repo1.png"
  alt="bitbucket-custom-repo1"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>
<Image
  src="/assets/images/insights/bitbucket/bitbucket-custom-repo2.png"
  alt="bitbucket-custom-repo2"
  width={1000}
  height={500}
/>

- If the workspace you want to proceed with exists on the dropdown, you can select it and proceed to selecting its repositories and branches.

- Finally Click **"Create"** button to finish your configuration.
